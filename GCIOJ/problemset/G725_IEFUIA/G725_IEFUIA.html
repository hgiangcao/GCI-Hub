The exam contains 2 part: Paper question and Coding. Paper quetions score take 70% of the score, while Coding take 30% of the score

Take the paper quetions score and coding score from keyboard as paper_score and coding_score

Your task is to calculate final_score and print to screen

Example 1:

Input: paper_score = 100; coding_score = 100
Screen output: 100

Example 2:

Input: paper_score = 70; coding_score = 100
Screen output: 79
